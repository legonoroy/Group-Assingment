How do I place an order?
Orders are placed via our website. Should you have an issue placing an order, you could also reach out to our friendly Customer Experience team at +65-44446677. We would be most happy to assist by placing an order via phone for you.

Can I still change my address, or cancel my order, after the order is confirmed?
If you have realised an error in your order or changed your mind shortly after placing your order, please contact our Customer Experience Team at +65-44446677 (Monday to Sunday, 9am - 6pm) and we will try to modify your order accordingly. If more than two hours have passed, chances are good that your order is ready to be shipped! In either case, please give us a call for further assistance as soon as possible.

What is your shipping policy?
At FRIAS, we aim to deliver your order to you within 1 to 3, or 7 to 14 working days, from the day you receive the Order Shipped email notification. The delivery time estimate depends on the availability of the items at the warehouse, our courier partners' dispatching schedules and routing, as well as traffic and weather conditions.

What is your Returns policy?
Change your mind about your purchase? No problem! Simply return your items within 30 days from the day you receive them, unused with tags intact and in their original brand packaging.

My order is not here yet. What should I do?
Please reach out to our Customer Experience Team at Frias@gmail.com, or at +65-44446677 with your Order ID and shipping address for speedy assistance!

What is the delivery lead time for my order?
We aim to get your order to you within 3 working days from the date of your order.

What forms of payment methods do you offer?
At FRIAS, you can easily make payment via PayPal
